$$ Art Materials Charges $$

An Art Materials Charge will be collected in all art courses, grades 6 through 12. The prevailing sales tax has been included in each Art Materials Charge. The Art Materials Charge will be used to purchase materials of a select nature used in projects that the student makes and may choose to take home. If a student purchased the same materials provided by the Art Materials Charge, the student would have to pay the retail price, which is approximately two (2) times the price paid by the school through bulk rate purchasing. The list of materials provided through the Art Materials Charge is available through the Art teacher. These materials include - but may not be limited to - the following:

  • Charcoal/Conte Crayons
  • Clay/Grog/Cones
  • Drawing pencils
  • Drawing/Painting/Printmaking papers
  • Dyes
  • Fabrics
  • Felt tip pens/markers
  • India ink
  • Papier mache/Art paste
  • Woodblocks or Linoleum
  • Pastels/Chalks
  • Photography chemicals/supplies
  • Printing inks
  • Stains/Enamels
  • Tape/Masking
  • Tempera/Acrylic paint
  • Tissue paper
  • Wire
  • Yarn/Jute/Burlap
